Privacy Policy for Fomo Screener

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

Fomo Screener is a browser extension that helps users quickly navigate from Solana token contract addresses to their corresponding Dexscreener analysis pages. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

What We DON'T Collect:

  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • Browsing history or website content
  • Financial information or wallet data
  • Any data from web pages you visit
  • User account information

What We DO Collect:

  • Local Settings: Your extension preferences (notification settings, toggle states)
  • Usage Statistics: Basic counts of how many times you've used the extension (stored locally only)
  • Selected Text: Temporarily processed to identify Solana addresses (not stored or transmitted)

How We Use Any Information We Collect

Local Storage Only:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
  • Settings and statistics never leave your browser
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties

Text Processing:

  • When you select text on a webpage, we temporarily analyze it to detect Solana token addresses
  • This processing happens entirely within your browser
  • Selected text is never stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. All functionality operates locally within your browser.

Third-Party Services

  • The extension opens links to Dexscreener.com when you use its core functionality
  • We do not control Dexscreener's privacy practices in any way (please review their privacy policy separately)
  • No data is shared with Dexscreener beyond the standard web navigation

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab: Allows reading selected text on the current tab to identify Solana addresses
  • contextMenus: Adds right-click menu options for convenience
  • storage: Stores your preferences locally on your device
  • tabs: Opens new tabs with Dexscreener links
  • notifications: Shows optional success/error messages
  • host permissions: Enables the extension to work on all websites where you might find Solana addresses

Your Rights

  • Delete Data: Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data
  • Control Settings: Disable notifications or other features in the extension popup
  • Transparency: All code functionality is contained within the extension package

Data Security

  • All data remains on your local device
  • No external data transmission occurs
  • Chrome's built-in security protects your local storage

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Significant changes will be reflected in the extension version and store listing.

Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us via X/Twitter DMs.

Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws
